About our work

Rooted in
intention

Our work is rooted in intention. We focus on details, direction, and visuals that feel clean, natural, and aligned. We believe great content doesn't need to be loud — it needs to be clear.

We collaborate closely, move fast, and adapt easily. Whether it's ongoing content, a campaign, or an event, we approach every project with the same mindset: make it make sense, visually and creatively.

About the Founders

Built through
creative + execution

Cerri Media was founded in 2023 by Luiza and Breno Cerri, Brazilian-American creatives with different backgrounds and a shared eye for visual storytelling.

Coming from different professional backgrounds, we both always shared a strong creative instinct. Breno was naturally drawn to photography and filmmaking, while Luiza focused on videography, direction, and brand storytelling. Over time, those perspectives naturally evolved into a shared creative vision that became the foundation of Cerri Media.

Our styles align, but also complement each other. Clients often connect with our work because of the visuals, the rhythm, the details, and the feeling behind each project, and stay because of how intentional and collaborative the process feels.

As the company grew, we built a team that reflects that same approach. Every creative we work with is carefully selected to align with our standards, aesthetic, and way of creating.

We stay hands-on from start to finish, making sure every project feels cohesive, thoughtful, and aligned with the brand's vision.
